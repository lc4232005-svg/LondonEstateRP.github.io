Server Rules

Please read and follow all rules to ensure a great roleplay experience

FiveM Terms of Service

1. Respect FiveM Guidelines

All players must adhere to the official FiveM Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Any violation of FiveM's TOS will result in immediate action.

2. No Cheating or Exploiting

The use of cheats, hacks, mods, or any third-party software that provides an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. Exploiting bugs or glitches is also forbidden.

3. Respect Intellectual Property

Do not use copyrighted material without proper authorization. This includes but is not limited to music, images, and brand assets.

4. No Harassment or Hate Speech

Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or any form of toxic behavior towards other players based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or any other characteristic is strictly prohibited.

5. Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old to play on this server. By joining, you confirm that you meet this age requirement.

General Roleplay Rules

VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch)

Using your vehicle as a weapon to kill or injure players without valid roleplay reason is prohibited. Vehicles should not be used to run over players unless it's a life-threatening situation with proper RP initiation.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

RDM (Random Deathmatch)

Killing or attacking other players without valid roleplay reason or proper initiation is strictly forbidden. All hostile actions must have proper RP context and escalation.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Metagaming

Using out-of-character (OOC) information for in-character (IC) advantage is not allowed. This includes using Discord, streams, or other external sources to gain IC information.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Powergaming

Forcing actions upon other players without giving them a chance to respond or performing unrealistic actions that your character couldn't do in real life is prohibited.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

New Life Rule (NLR)

After being killed and respawning at a hospital, you lose all memory of the events leading to your death. You cannot return to the location of your death for 15 minutes or seek revenge.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Value of Life (VOL)

Your character's life is valuable. You must act realistically in life-threatening situations. If someone has a weapon pointed at you, you must comply. You cannot pull a weapon when already under gunpoint.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Combat & Engagement Rules

Proper Initiation

All hostile actions must be properly initiated with clear communication. You must give the other player a reasonable chance to comply before escalating to violence.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Safe Zones

No hostile actions are permitted in safe zones including: Police Department, Hospital, Garages, and Civilian Spawn areas. Crime cannot be committed in these areas.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Drive-by Shooting

Shooting from a vehicle as a passenger is allowed only if the vehicle is stationary. The driver cannot shoot while driving.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Combat Logging

Leaving the server during an active roleplay situation, while being chased, or while in custody is strictly prohibited. You must wait until the situation is resolved.

Punishment: Ban

Police & Emergency Services

Respect EMS

Emergency Medical Services must be respected at all times. Do not interfere with EMS operations, steal from injured players, or attack EMS personnel.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Police Roleplay

All interactions with police must be roleplayed properly. You cannot ignore police, run without reason, or fail to comply with reasonable orders.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

Corruption

Police corruption is allowed only with prior approval from server administration. Unauthorized corrupt behavior will result in removal from police faction.

Punishment: Faction Removal → Ban

Vehicle Rules

Realistic Driving

Drive realistically at all times. Excessive speeding, reckless driving, or using vehicles as weapons is prohibited unless in a proper RP scenario.

Punishment: Warning → Vehicle Impound

Vehicle Storage

Vehicles must be parked properly. Do not abandon vehicles in the middle of roads or in unrealistic locations. Vehicles left unattended may be impounded.

Punishment: Vehicle Impound

Communication Rules

In-Character Chat

Use IC chat for all in-character communication. OOC chat should only be used for out-of-character discussions. Keep OOC to a minimum.

Punishment: Warning → Mute

No Toxic Behavior

Toxic behavior, excessive swearing, harassment, or inappropriate language in any chat channel is prohibited. Be respectful to all players.

Punishment: Warning → Mute → Ban

No Advertising

Advertising other servers, Discord communities, or services is strictly prohibited.

Punishment: Permanent Ban

Exploits & Bugs

No Exploiting

Using any bug, glitch, or exploit to gain an advantage is forbidden. If you discover a bug, report it to staff immediately.

Punishment: Warning → Ban

No Money Glitches

Any method of obtaining money through bugs, glitches, or exploits is strictly prohibited. All illegitimate funds will be removed.

Punishment: Money Reset → Ban

Final Notes

Staff Discretion

Staff members reserve the right to punish any behavior they deem inappropriate, even if not explicitly listed in these rules. Use common sense and respect others.

Rule Updates

Rules are subject to change at any time. It is your responsibility to stay updated on the latest rules by checking this page regularly.

Appeals

If you believe you were punished unfairly, you can appeal on our Discord server. Provide evidence and be respectful in your appeal.

By playing on London Estate RP, you agree to follow all rules listed above.
